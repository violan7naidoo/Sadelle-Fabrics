About Us

History

Sadelle Fabrics has been one of the most trusted and loved brands since it first opened its doors in 2019. A passion for fabric and unique designs is what drives it to create incredible pieces.

After realising there was a need for affordable yet quality materials, the founder of Sadelle Fabrics, Sajida Pillay, decided to share her passion with others. It is a local brand committed to supplying only the best.

Mission & Vision

Focused on distributing fabrics for several industries including, the clothing, catering, curtaining and upholstery industries, as well as your home. Sadelle Fabrics has committed itself to supply only the best.

Every piece of fabric is unique in design which is regularly updated and renewed. Giving its range advantage over the rest. Specific fabrics that may be requested may also be outsourced.
